FaceTogether

Conceived as our team's final project, FaceTogether embodies a comprehensive full-stack application, integrating HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, MySQL, Java, and Spring Boot technologies. Centered around the concept of inclusivity, our platform serves as a skincare store dedicated to catering to the diverse needs of a broad audience. With a keen focus on providing skincare solutions tailored to all skin types, FaceTogether strives to create an accessible and user-friendly experience. From gentle moisturizers to targeted treatments, our curated selection of products is designed to meet the unique requirements of individuals from every background, fostering a community where everyone can confidently embark on their skincare journey.
